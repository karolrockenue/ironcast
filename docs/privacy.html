IRONCAST

Privacy Policy · Effective 20 April 2026

SUMMARY

IronCast does not collect, transmit, sell, rent, or share any data. The app has no server, no account system, no analytics, and no advertising. Your workout data never leaves your device.

WHAT WE COLLECT

Nothing. The app has no network calls of any kind.

WHAT WE STORE

Your workout history (exercises, sets, weights, reps, timestamps) is stored locally on your device in a single SQLite database file. Deleting the app permanently deletes this data. There is no backup, export, or recovery mechanism.

PERMISSIONS

IronCast requests local-notification permission so the rest timer can alert you when your rest is up, including when the app is backgrounded or the screen is locked. These notifications are scheduled and fired locally on your device; nothing is sent to a server.

THIRD PARTIES

None. No SDKs, no trackers, no advertising networks, no cloud storage providers, no crash reporters.

CHILDREN

IronCast is a fitness-logging utility with no social features and collects no personal data. It does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

CHANGES

If this policy changes, the "Effective" date above will be updated and the new policy will apply from that date. Material changes will be announced via an in-app notice in a future release.
